Important FAQ

What is a flood?

A flood is an overflow of water that submerges land, often caused by heavy rain, river overflow, or dam breaks.

How do floods impact communities?

Floods damage homes, infrastructure, agriculture, and can lead to loss of life, displacement, and waterborne diseases.

How can people prepare for a flood?

People can prepare by creating an emergency plan, storing supplies, elevating important belongings, and staying informed about weather conditions.

What are common causes of floods?

Common causes include heavy rainfall, river overflow, coastal storms, and failure of water control structures like dams.
